## Description

**Transform your music production with the power of AI.**

Echoelmusic is a revolutionary music production platform that combines cutting-edge artificial intelligence with professional audio tools. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned producer, Echoelmusic empowers you to create stunning music with unprecedented ease and creativity.

### AI-Powered Features

**Intelligent Harmonizer**
Our Super Intelligent Harmonizer uses advanced voice leading algorithms based on Tymoczko geometry and Huron cognitive principles to create perfect harmonies in real-time. Choose from 80+ voice characters including choir, synthesizers, and acoustic instruments.

**AI Sub-Agents**
Let autonomous AI agents handle complex tasks. Our multi-agent system includes specialized Composers, Arrangers, Mixers, Mastering Engineers, and more - all working together to bring your vision to life.

**Neural Stem Separation**
Extract vocals, drums, bass, and other elements from any audio with state-of-the-art neural network technology. Powered by HT-Demucs for industry-leading quality.

### Professional Tools

**Plugin Support**
Full support for Audio Unit (AU), AUv3, and VST3 plugins. Use your favorite effects and instruments alongside our built-in tools.

**VJ Mode**
Take your visuals live with our full-featured VJ mode. Real-time video layers, audio-reactive effects, MIDI mapping, and professional output controls.

**Physics Visualizer**
Create mesmerizing visuals with our GPU-accelerated particle physics engine. Antigravity, vortex, attractor forces - all reactive to your music.

**Workflow Automation**
Connect to n8n for powerful workflow automation. Automatically backup projects, post to social media, and more.
