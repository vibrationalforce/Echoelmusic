## ‚ö†Ô∏è What NOT to Do

### ‚ùå Don't: Subscription Only

**Why it fails:**
- 62% of users prefer one-time payment
- Subscription fatigue is real
- Missing out on "ownership" psychology
- No upfront cash injection

### ‚ùå Don't: Lifetime Only

**Why it fails:**
- No recurring revenue (unpredictable)
- Can't fund ongoing development
- Servers/infrastructure cost monthly
- Hard to scale

### ‚ùå Don't: High Revenue Share (15-20%)

**Why it fails:**
- Fiverr/Upwork already do this (15-20%)
- Musicians will use platforms with lower fees
- 5-10% is competitive advantage
- Higher commission = fewer freelancers

### ‚ùå Don't: Complicated Pricing

**Bad example:**
```
Basic: $4.99
Plus: $9.99
Premium: $14.99
Ultra: $24.99
Mega: $49.99
```

**TOO MANY OPTIONS = DECISION PARALYSIS**

Keep it simple: Free, Pro, Lifetime, Premium
