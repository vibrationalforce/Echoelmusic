## Information We Collect

### 1. Health & Fitness Data (HealthKit)

**What We Collect:**
- Heart rate (BPM)
- Heart rate variability (HRV)
- Workout data (duration, type)
- Movement and activity metrics

**Why We Collect It:**
- To create bio-reactive music that responds to your physiological state
- To map heart rate to tempo and musical parameters
- To provide real-time biofeedback during music creation
- To enable wellness and meditation features

**How We Use It:**
- All health data is processed **locally on your device**
- Health data is **NEVER** uploaded to our servers
- Health data is **NEVER** shared with third parties
- Data is used **only** for real-time audio parameter mapping
- No health data is stored permanently

**Your Control:**
- You can revoke HealthKit permissions at any time in iOS Settings
- The app will continue to work without HealthKit (bio-reactive features disabled)
- No health data leaves your device

### 2. Microphone Access

**What We Collect:**
- Real-time audio input from your device microphone

**Why We Collect It:**
- To record audio for music production
- To enable live audio effects processing
- To detect pitch and rhythm for musical features

**How We Use It:**
- Audio is processed in real-time for recording and effects
- Recordings are stored **locally on your device**
- Microphone data is **NEVER** transmitted to our servers
- No voice recognition or speech processing occurs

### 3. Camera Access

**What We Collect:**
- Camera frames for face tracking
- Video recording for music video creation

**Why We Collect It:**
- To detect facial expressions for audio parameter control
- To record music videos
- To enable AR-based musical interactions

**How We Use It:**
- Face tracking is processed **locally on your device**
- Camera data is **NEVER** uploaded to our servers
- Videos are stored **only** in your photo library (with your permission)

### 4. Photo Library Access

**What We Collect:**
- Access to save exported audio and video files

**Why We Collect It:**
- To save your music productions to your photo library
- To export session recordings

**How We Use It:**
- We **only write** to your photo library (save files)
- We **do not read** existing photos without explicit permission
- All exports are user-initiated

### 5. Bluetooth Access

**What We Collect:**
- Connection to Bluetooth MIDI controllers
- Connection to Bluetooth heart rate monitors
- Connection to Bluetooth audio devices

**Why We Collect It:**
- To enable MIDI controller input for music production
- To connect wireless heart rate sensors for bio-reactive features
- To optimize audio output for Bluetooth headphones/speakers

**How We Use It:**
- Only music-related Bluetooth devices are accessed
- No location tracking via Bluetooth
- No data transmission to Bluetooth devices except audio/MIDI signals

### 6. Motion & Orientation Data

**What We Collect:**
- Device motion and orientation sensors

**Why We Collect It:**
- To enable head tracking for spatial audio
- To detect device movement for gestural music control

**How We Use It:**
- Motion data is processed **locally on your device**
- Used **only** for audio spatialization and gesture recognition
- **NEVER** transmitted to servers

## Your Rights & Control

### You Have Complete Control:

1. **Permissions Management**
   - Grant or revoke microphone access: iOS Settings → Echoelmusic → Microphone
   - Grant or revoke camera access: iOS Settings → Echoelmusic → Camera
   - Grant or revoke HealthKit access: Health app → Sources → Echoelmusic
   - Grant or revoke photo library access: iOS Settings → Echoelmusic → Photos

2. **Data Deletion**
   - Delete all app data: iOS Settings → Echoelmusic → Delete App
   - Delete specific projects: Inside the app, swipe to delete
   - Clear all sessions: App → Sessions → Edit → Delete All

3. **Export Your Data**
   - Export all projects via App → Export → Backup
   - All data is in standard formats (WAV, MIDI, etc.)

### No Account Required

- No login or account creation
- No email or password
- No user profile
- Complete privacy by design
