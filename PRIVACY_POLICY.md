## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Data You Provide Directly

#### **Account Information**
- Email address (required for account creation)
- Username (public, chosen by you)
- Profile picture (optional)
- Portfolio information (optional, for EoelWork)

#### **User-Generated Content**
- Audio recordings you create
- Video recordings you create
- Project files and sessions
- EoelWork gig postings and applications
- Reviews and ratings

### 1.2 Data Collected Automatically

#### **Device Information (Required)**
- **Purpose:** Performance optimization and compatibility
- **Data Collected:**
  - Device model (e.g., iPhone 15 Pro)
  - Operating system version (e.g., iOS 17.0)
  - App version
  - Screen resolution
  - Available storage space
- **Retention:** 90 days
- **Third Parties:** None
- **Legal Basis:** Legitimate interest (app functionality)

#### **Usage Data (Optional - Requires Consent)**
- **Purpose:** App improvement and feature development
- **Data Collected:**
  - Feature usage statistics
  - App launch times
  - Crash reports (if consent given)
  - Performance metrics
- **Retention:** 90 days
- **Third Parties:** TelemetryDeck (privacy-friendly analytics)
- **Legal Basis:** Consent
- **Opt-out:** Settings > Privacy > Analytics

### 1.3 Sensitive Data (Optional - Requires Explicit Consent)

#### **Health Data (HealthKit)**
- **Purpose:** Adaptive audio-visual experiences based on biometrics
- **Data Collected:**
  - Heart rate variability (HRV)
  - Heart rate
  - Respiratory rate
- **Processing:** Real-time processing only, NOT stored permanently
- **Retention:** Immediate processing, not stored
- **Third Parties:** None
- **Legal Basis:** Explicit consent
- **Opt-out:** Settings > Privacy > Health Data

#### **Biometric Data (ARKit Face Tracking)**
- **Purpose:** Facial expression mapping to audio parameters
- **Data Collected:**
  - Facial landmarks (52 points)
  - Head position and rotation
- **Processing:** Real-time processing only, NOT stored
- **Retention:** Not stored (processed in real-time only)
- **Third Parties:** None
- **Legal Basis:** Explicit consent
- **Note:** This is NOT Face ID authentication. We use ARKit for creative control only.

#### **Audio Data**
- **Purpose:** Audio recording, processing, and music production
- **Data Collected:**
  - Microphone input
  - Recorded audio files
- **Retention:** Until you delete them (stored locally on your device)
- **Third Parties:** None (unless you export and share)
- **Encryption:** All recordings encrypted at rest (AES-256)

#### **Location Data (Optional)**
- **Purpose:** EoelWork gig matching based on proximity
- **Data Collected:**
  - Approximate location (city/region level)
  - GPS coordinates (only when using EoelWork)
- **Retention:** 30 days
- **Third Parties:** Firebase (infrastructure only)
- **Legal Basis:** Consent
- **Opt-out:** Settings > Privacy > Location

### 1.4 Payment Information

- **Purpose:** EoelWork payments and subscriptions
- **Data Collected:**
  - Payment card details (last 4 digits only)
  - Billing address
  - Transaction history
- **Processing:** Handled by Stripe (PCI-DSS compliant)
- **Retention:** Transaction records: 7 years (legal requirement)
- **Third Parties:** Stripe Payment Processing
- **We NEVER store full credit card numbers**

## 4. Your Privacy Rights

‚úÖ **Right to Access** (Article 15)
- Request a copy of all data we hold about you
- **How to exercise:** Settings > Privacy > Export My Data

‚úÖ **Right to Rectification** (Article 16)
- Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
- **How to exercise:** Settings > Profile > Edit

‚úÖ **Right to Erasure** (Article 17)
- Delete all your data ("Right to be Forgotten")
- **How to exercise:** Settings > Privacy > Delete My Data
- **Timeline:** Immediate deletion from our systems

‚úÖ **Right to Data Portability** (Article 20)
- Export your data in machine-readable format (JSON)
- **How to exercise:** Settings > Privacy > Export My Data

‚úÖ **Right to Restriction** (Article 18)
- Limit how we process your data
- **How to exercise:** Settings > Privacy > Restrict Processing

‚úÖ **Right to Object** (Article 21)
- Object to specific data processing
- **How to exercise:** Settings > Privacy > Object to Processing

‚úÖ **Right to Withdraw Consent** (Article 7)

### 4.2 CCPA Rights (California Users)
