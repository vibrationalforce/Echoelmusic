## üìä Information We Collect

### **1. Health and Biometric Data**

Echoelmusic uses HealthKit to access and process health data for bio-reactive audio features:

**Data Types Collected:**
- ‚úÖ **Heart Rate (BPM)** - For real-time audio parameter control
- ‚úÖ **Heart Rate Variability (HRV)** - For stress/flow state detection
- ‚úÖ **Respiratory Rate** - For breathing guidance features
- ‚úÖ **Blood Oxygen Saturation (SpO2)** - Optional for advanced biofeedback
- ‚úÖ **Body Temperature** - Optional for physiological monitoring

**How We Use This Data:**
- Map biometric signals to audio/visual parameters in real-time
- Calculate HRV coherence scores for flow state detection
- Provide personalized breathing guidance
- Track wellness progress over time (locally on your device)

**Data Storage:**
- **All health data is stored ONLY on your device** via HealthKit
- We do NOT upload health data to our servers
- We do NOT share health data with third parties
- Data can be deleted anytime via iOS Settings ‚Üí Health ‚Üí Data Access & Devices

**Your Rights:**
- You can revoke HealthKit access at any time
- Revoking access will limit bio-reactive features but the app will remain functional
- You control what data the app can read/write via iOS HealthKit permissions

### **2. Camera and Microphone Data**

**Microphone Access:**
- Used for voice/audio input, pitch detection, and audio recording
- Audio is processed locally for real-time effects
- Recordings are saved only when you explicitly record a track
- Audio data is never uploaded without your explicit consent
