3. Biometric Data Disclaimer

Biometric data (heart rate, HRV, breathing) displayed in the App is FOR INFORMATIONAL AND CREATIVE PURPOSES ONLY.

This data is:

  • NOT accurate enough for medical decisions

  • Dependent on third-party device accuracy
  • Subject to interference and errors

Do NOT rely on App biometric data for medical diagnosis, treatment decisions, or monitoring chronic health conditions.

4. When to Consult a Doctor

Consult a qualified healthcare professional:

  • BEFORE using the App if you have any medical conditions, especially cardiovascular disease, epilepsy, respiratory conditions, mental health conditions, pregnancy, or implanted medical devices
  • IF you experience chest pain, shortness of breath, dizziness, irregular heartbeat, severe headache, seizures, or any concerning symptoms
  • BEFORE making any health-related decisions based on App use

5. Specific Feature Warnings

⚠ Photosensitive Seizure Warning

Visual effects in this App may trigger SEIZURES in individuals with photosensitive epilepsy. STOP using the App immediately if you experience seizures, loss of awareness, eye twitching, or disorientation.

Breathing Exercises

STOP breathing exercises immediately if you feel dizzy, lightheaded, experience chest pain, or feel anxious. Consult your doctor before use if you have respiratory conditions.

VR/AR Features

Virtual and augmented reality features may cause motion sickness, dizziness, eye strain, or headaches. Remove headset if you experience discomfort.

Audio Safety

Protect your hearing. Use reasonable volume levels and take breaks from headphone use.

6. Contraindications

DO NOT use the App (or consult your doctor first) if you have:

  • Pacemaker or implanted cardioverter-defibrillator (ICD)
  • Other implanted electronic medical devices
  • Epilepsy or seizure disorders
  • Severe cardiovascular disease
  • Severe respiratory disease
  • Severe mental illness
  • Pregnancy (for certain features)

7. Emergency Situations

IF YOU EXPERIENCE A MEDICAL EMERGENCY:

1. STOP using the App immediately
2. CALL emergency services (911 in US, 112 in EU)
3. Follow emergency responder instructions

The App CANNOT detect medical emergencies, provide emergency medical guidance, or contact emergency services on your behalf.
