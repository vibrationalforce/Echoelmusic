Health Disclaimer

Updated: February 2026

Important: Not a Medical Device

Echoelmusic is a creative audio-visual tool, NOT a medical device. It is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease or medical condition. Always consult qualified healthcare professionals for medical advice.

Biofeedback Features

Echoelmusic uses biometric data (heart rate, HRV, breathing patterns) for creative and general wellness purposes only. These readings are approximate and should not be used for medical monitoring.

  • Do not rely on this app for health decisions
  • HRV and coherence metrics are for creative inspiration only
  • Consult a doctor before beginning any wellness program

Photosensitivity Warning

Some visual effects include flashing lights, rapid color changes, and strobing patterns. If you have photosensitive epilepsy, a history of seizures, or are prone to light-induced discomfort, consult a physician before use. Stop using immediately if you experience discomfort.

Accessibility Options

We provide settings to reduce or disable potentially triggering effects:

  • Reduced Motion: Disables animations and rapid transitions
  • No Strobing: Removes all flashing light effects
  • Low Contrast: Softens visual intensity
  • Audio-Only Mode: Disables all visual elements

Hearing Safety

Prolonged exposure to loud audio can damage hearing. Use moderate volume levels. Take breaks during extended sessions. If you experience ringing in ears or discomfort, reduce volume immediately.

DMX & Laser Safety

When using lighting control features:

  • Follow all local regulations for laser use
  • Use appropriate safety zones for laser effects
  • Never point lasers at people or reflective surfaces
  • Ensure proper ventilation when using fog/haze machines

For Wellness Coaches & Therapists: If you use Echoelmusic in professional practice, ensure clients understand this is a creative tool, not a therapeutic device. It does not replace clinical biofeedback equipment or professional treatment.

Emergency

If you experience any adverse reactions while using Echoelmusic, stop using the app immediately and seek medical attention if necessary.
