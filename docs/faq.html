FAQ

Everything you need to know about Echoelmusic

General

What is Echoelmusic?
Echoelmusic is a bio-reactive creative platform that transforms your biometric signals (heart rate, HRV, breathing) into music, visuals, and lighting in real-time. Available on iPhone, iPad, Mac, Apple Watch, Apple TV, and Vision Pro.
What makes it "bio-reactive"?
Bio-reactive means the software responds to your body in real time. EchoelBio captures your biometrics — HRV, heart rate, breath, face tracking, EEG, and movement — and feeds them to every other tool via EngineBus. Your heart rate influences tempo and filter brightness, HRV modulates reverb and effects depth, breathing shapes delay and spatial audio, and your coherence level affects harmony, warmth, and saturation. Your body becomes the controller.
Do I need special hardware?
For basic use, just your iPhone or iPad. Bio-reactive features work with your iPhone's front camera (face tracking for pulse and expression) or an Apple Watch / Bluetooth heart rate monitor for deeper biometric data. For lighting, Echoelmusic controls smart home lights, DMX fixtures, and Art-Net networks — from your living room to the stage. For laser visuals, any ILDA-compatible controller works. For MIDI, any class-compliant controller connects instantly.
What are the 10 EchoelTools?
All of Echoelmusic's 498 engines are consolidated into 10 unified tools, connected via EngineBus:
  • EchoelSynth — 7 synthesis engines (DDSP, Modal, Cellular, Quantum, Sampler, TR-808, Spectral Morph)
  • EchoelFX — 20+ effects & analog emulations (EQ, Comp, Reverb, Delay, Tube, FET, VCA)
  • EchoelMix — Console mixer, multi-track recording, session management & metering
  • EchoelSeq — Step sequencer, pattern bank, automation & Lambda scripting
  • EchoelMIDI — MIDI 2.0, MPE, touch instruments (keys, pads, drums, strum, Audio→MIDI)
  • EchoelBio — HRV, heart rate, breath, face tracking, EEG & signal deconvolution
  • EchoelField — Visuals, video, NLE editor, ProRes, avatars, procedural worlds & 6 biomes
  • EchoelBeam — DMX/Art-Net lighting, lasers, cue system, external displays, projection & VR/XR
  • EchoelNet — 17+ protocols (OSC, MSC, Dante, EchoelSync), SharePlay, cloud sync & dynamic NFTs
  • EchoelMind — On-device 3B-param CoreML intelligence, 20+ languages, voice assistant & adaptive learning
Fewer tools, more power. Every tool talks to every other tool in real time.

Instruments & Synthesis

What synthesis engines are included?
All synthesis is unified inside EchoelSynth — one tool with 6 pluggable backends:
  • DDSP — Differentiable DSP (harmonic + noise model, ML-powered)
  • Modal — Physics-constrained modal resonator (bells, plates, strings, gongs)
  • Cellular — Cellular automata → audio synthesis
  • Quantum — Schrödinger wavefunction synthesis (EchoelQuant)
  • Sampler — Sample playback, slicing & manipulation
  • TR-808 — Classic drum machine + bass synth
Plus binaural beat generation, 45+ parametric factory presets across 7 categories, and bio-reactive synthesis that maps your biometrics directly to sound parameters in real time. All connected via EngineBus.
What touch instruments are available?
  • Chord Pad — 11 chord types, 13 scales, strum & arpeggio modes
  • Drum Pad — 16-pad grid with 6 kits (Acoustic, Electronic, TR-808, TR-909, Hip Hop, Percussion)
  • Melody XY Pad — Continuous 2D pitch/timbre control with scale quantization
  • Keyboard — Multi-octave piano with velocity & pitch bend

All touch instruments support MIDI 2.0 with MPE (per-note expression).
Are there orchestral instruments?

Composing & Music Creation

Does Echoelmusic compose music for me?
No — and that's by design. Echoelmusic is a creativity tool, not an AI that generates finished pieces. EchoelMind helps you learn different musical styles, explore scales and chord progressions, and develop your own unique melodies and compositions. Your biometric data guides the musical parameters — tempo from your heart rate, harmonic variation from your HRV, dynamic expression from your breathing — but you are always the creator. We evolve your creativity, we don't replace it.
What composing tools are available?
EchoelSeq provides the sequencer and automation layer. EchoelMind adds intelligent composition assistance:
  • Step Sequencer — Up to 64 steps with probability, velocity per step, pattern bank with 128 slots, and Lambda scripting for generative sequences
  • Biometric Music Generator — Uses Markov chains and music theory to suggest melodies based on 18 scales and your bio-data. You shape the output.
  • Film Score Composer — Learn 17 cinematic scene types and 21 compositional techniques (leitmotif, ostinato, fanfare, etc.) with 9 harmonic progression styles.
  • Music Theory Database — Explore 13 global music cultures: Western, Indian Raga, Arabic Maqam, Japanese, Gamelan, Flamenco, Blues/Jazz, and more.
  • Brainwave Entrainment — Delta, Theta, Alpha, Beta, Gamma presets for focus, relaxation, and creativity.

MIDI & Hardware

What MIDI features are supported?
All MIDI lives inside EchoelMIDI. Full MIDI 2.0 with Universal MIDI Packets (32-bit resolution), MPE (MIDI Polyphonic Expression) for per-note control, and inter-app tempo sync for beat-locked sessions with other music apps and devices. Any class-compliant MIDI controller works. Controllers with RGB LED grids get dedicated bio-reactive feedback patterns. Real-time Audio→MIDI pitch detection lets you sing or play any instrument to generate MIDI notes.
What about recording and export?
EchoelMix handles recording and session management. 25+ export formats including WAV, FLAC, AAC, MP3, Opus, ProRes, H.264/H.265, Dolby Digital, Dolby Atmos (7.1.4), and DTS:X. Export presets for streaming services, social media, broadcast, cinema, and archival masters. Multi-track recording with punch-in, loop recording, and automatic take management.
Does Echoelmusic work as an AUv3 plugin and host?
Both. Echoelmusic provides AUv3 Audio Unit plugins (808 Bass Synth, BioComposer, Stem Splitter, MIDI Pro) that you can load inside Logic Pro, GarageBand, AUM, or any AUv3-compatible host. At the same time, Echoelmusic itself is an AUv3 host — load third-party AUv3 instruments and effects directly into the bio-reactive signal chain.

Health & Privacy

Is this a medical device?
No. Echoelmusic is a creative tool, not a medical device. Biofeedback features (heart rate, HRV, coherence scores) are for creative and general wellness purposes only. They are not clinically validated measurements. Always consult healthcare professionals for medical advice. See our Health Disclaimer.
Photosensitivity warnings?
Some visual effects include flashing lights, rapid color changes, and laser patterns. If you have photosensitive epilepsy or seizure conditions, consult a physician before use. Accessibility options can reduce or disable motion and flashing effects.
Is my data safe?
All biometric data is processed locally on your device. We don't collect, store, or transmit personal data. No accounts, no analytics, no third-party SDKs. Platform-native encryption (AES-256) protects local storage. See our Privacy Policy and Security page.
