Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Echoelmusic

General

What is Echoelmusic?
Echoelmusic is a professional bio-reactive audio-visual production suite that transforms your biometric signals (heart rate, HRV, breathing, gestures) into music, visuals, and lighting in real-time. Available for iOS, Android, macOS, Windows, and Linux.
What makes it "bio-reactive"?
Bio-reactive means the software responds to your body signals. Your heart rate controls tempo, HRV modulates filters, breathing shapes reverb, and coherence levels affect harmony. Your physiological state directly influences the art you create.
Do I need special hardware?
For basic use, just a compatible device. For bio-reactive features, you need a heart rate monitor (Apple Watch, Garmin, Whoop, or Bluetooth HR sensor). For lighting, DMX-compatible fixtures or Art-Net devices.

Features & Compatibility

  • visionOS — Vision Pro
  • Android 8+ — Phones & Tablets
  • Windows 10+ — x64
  • Linux — x64, ARM64
What plugin formats are available?
VST3, AU (Audio Units), AAX (Pro Tools), AUv3 (iOS/macOS), CLAP, and LV2. Compatible with Ableton Live, Logic Pro, Pro Tools, FL Studio, Reaper, Cubase, and most DAWs.
What wearables work with Echoelmusic?
Apple Watch (via HealthKit), Garmin, Whoop, Polar, Oura Ring, Fitbit, and any Bluetooth Low Energy heart rate monitor. Also supports professional ECG devices via OSC.
Can I control lights and lasers?
Yes! Full DMX512, Art-Net, and sACN support for professional lighting. ILDA laser control with safety zones. Moving heads, LED fixtures, fog machines — all bio-modulated.

Technical & Health

Is this a medical device?
No. Echoelmusic is a creative tool, not a medical device. Biofeedback features are for creative and wellness purposes only. Consult healthcare professionals for medical advice.
Photosensitivity warnings?
Yes. Some effects include flashing lights. If you have photosensitive epilepsy, consult a physician before use. Accessibility options can reduce or disable these effects.
Data privacy?
Biometric data stays on your device unless you explicitly share it. End-to-end encryption for cloud features. See our Privacy Policy.
