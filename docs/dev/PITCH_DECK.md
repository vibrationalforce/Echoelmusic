# Echoelmusic

## Bio-Reactive Audio Technology

---

## The Problem

Music production is **disconnected from the human body**.

- DAWs respond to mouse clicks, not heartbeats
- Electronic music feels mechanical, not alive
- Wellness apps have bad audio quality
- Live performance lacks biological authenticity

---

## The Solution

**Echoelmusic** makes music that **responds to your body**.

```
Your Heart Rate  →  Controls the Energy
Your Coherence   →  Shapes the Space
Your Stress      →  Drives the Intensity
Your Breath      →  Modulates the Flow
```
