Your body is the controller

Echoelmusic reads your heart rate, HRV, and breathing in real time — via your iPhone's front camera, Apple Watch, or any Bluetooth heart rate monitor. These signals drive everything — filters, reverb, tempo, visuals, and lighting. When you're in coherence, the sound opens up. When you breathe deep, the visuals expand. Play with touch instruments — chord pads, drum pads, keyboards, XY controllers, and strum pads — all responding to your body. It's your biology shaping art.
