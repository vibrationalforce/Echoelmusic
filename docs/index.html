Echoel

Music Producer & Artist

Music producer, sound artist and inventor of walk-in vibrating water, light and sound installations. Based in Hamburg. Creating interactive multisensory installations, video, visuals, light and musical performance since 2006 — most recently a vibration pontons and sound mirror workshop at Burgbeben STRNBRG 2024, created together with Anna.Elisie as Loewe Immerlieb. Active across multiple music projects with Tropical Drones collective.
