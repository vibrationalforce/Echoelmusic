Echoel

Music Producer

Michael Terbuyken is a musician, sound artist and creator of interactive multisensory installations, based in Hamburg since 2006. He studied Digital Media with a focus on sound at Hochschule Darmstadt (Media, Arts and Sciences) and continued with Musicology in Hamburg, working across the fields of art, science, medicine and technology.

Since 2009 he has been building interactive installations in public space that combine vibration, water, sound and light. Audiences are invited to move, feel and participate — the space itself becomes the instrument. His work has been shown at Documenta Kassel, Ars Electronica Linz, Fusion Festival, Kampnagel Hamburg, Golden Pudel Club, Kater Blau Berlin, Ritter Butzke Berlin, Garbicz Festival, the Canadian Embassy Berlin, Redlight Radio Amsterdam and many others.

In music production he moves freely between genres. Currently active in several projects spanning electronic music, autotune-driven future pop, and collaborative vegan food content.
