Privacy

Updated: February 2026

Summary: Your data stays on your device. We don't sell anything. You own your creations.

Data Collection

Biometric Data: Heart rate and HRV are processed locally on your device and never transmitted.

Audio: Microphone input for music production stays local.

Face Tracking: Facial expressions processed on-device for bio-reactive features.

No Servers

We don't operate servers for personal data. No accounts, no cloud storage for your biometrics.

HealthKit

Apple HealthKit data is never shared with third parties, never used for advertising, and never leaves your device.

Your Rights (GDPR)

  • Access, correction, deletion of your data
  • All data is local - delete app = data gone
  • Data portability: export your projects anytime
