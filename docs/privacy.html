1. Introduction

Welcome to Echoelmusic. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our bio-reactive audio-visual platform application.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with applicable data protection laws, including GDPR, CCPA, COPPA, PIPEDA, and Australian Privacy Principles.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Biometric and Health Data

The App processes biometric data to create bio-reactive audio-visual experiences:

  • Heart Rate Data from Apple Watch, HealthKit, or compatible wearables
  • Heart Rate Variability (HRV) for coherence calculations
  • Breathing Rate derived from HRV analysis
  • Facial Expression Data (processed on-device only)
  • Voice Data for voice-to-visual mapping (processed on-device, not stored)
  • Eye Gaze Data from visionOS devices (processed on-device only)
IMPORTANT: All biometric data is processed ON-DEVICE using Apple HealthKit, Android Health Connect, and local processing. Raw biometric data is NEVER transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly opt-in to cloud sync features.

2.2 Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information:

  • Device Information: Device type, OS version, unique device identifiers
  • App Usage: Features used, session duration, preset selections
  • Performance Metrics: App crashes, error logs
  • Network Data: IP address (anonymized), connection type

2.3 Children's Data (COPPA Compliance)

The App is NOT intended for children under 13 years of age (or under 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide Core Functionality: Process biometric data for bio-reactive experiences
  • Enhance User Experience: Remember preferences and custom presets
  • Improve the App: Analyze usage patterns (anonymized)
  • Provide Customer Support: Respond to inquiries
  • Ensure Security: Detect and prevent fraud

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement enterprise-grade security:

  • Encryption at Rest: AES-256 encryption
  • Encryption in Transit: TLS 1.3
  • Certificate Pinning: Prevents man-in-the-middle attacks
  • Biometric Authentication: Face ID, Touch ID, Optic ID
  • Secure Keychain Storage: iOS Keychain, Android Keystore

5. Data Sharing

We NEVER sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

We may share data with trusted service providers (cloud infrastructure, analytics, crash reporting) who are contractually bound to protect your data.

6. Your Rights

GDPR Rights (EU/EEA Users)

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive data in machine-readable format
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: At any time

CCPA Rights (California Residents)

  • Right to Know: Request disclosure of personal information
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: We don't sell data, but you can opt-out of analytics
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: Equal service regardless of privacy choices
