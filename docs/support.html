Frequently Asked Questions

What devices are supported?

Echoelmusic runs on iOS 15+, macOS 12+, watchOS 8+, tvOS 15+, visionOS 1+, Android 8+, Windows 10+, and Linux. Apple Watch is recommended for full biometric features, but the app works great with Garmin, Whoop, Oura, and other wearables too.

Is Echoelmusic a medical device?

No. Echoelmusic is NOT a medical device. It's designed for creative expression and general wellness only. It does not diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease. Always consult healthcare professionals for medical advice.

What's the pricing model?

99 lifetime license (one-time purchase) gives you access on all platforms. Major version upgrades (2.0, 3.0, etc.) are 49. The free TestFlight/beta program lets you try everything before buying.

Is my biometric data private?

Yes. All biometric data is processed locally on your device. Nothing is sent to our servers unless you explicitly enable cloud sync. We never sell your data. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Can I use it without a wearable?

Yes! Demo Mode simulates realistic biometric data so you can experience all features. You can also manually adjust parameters or use camera-based heart rate detection on supported devices.

What is HRV coherence?

Heart Rate Variability (HRV) coherence measures the regularity of your heartbeat intervals. In Echoelmusic, higher coherence creates more harmonious sounds and visuals. This is a creative tool, not medical measurement.

How do I report a bug?

Email michaelterbuyken@gmail.com with your device model, OS version, and steps to reproduce the issue. Screenshots or screen recordings help us fix things faster.

Refund policy?

Purchases through App Store or Play Store follow their respective refund policies. Contact Apple or Google directly for refund requests. We recommend trying the free beta first.
