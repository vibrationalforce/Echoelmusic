Frequently Asked Questions

What devices are supported?

Echoelmusic works on iOS 15+, macOS 12+, watchOS 8+, tvOS 15+, visionOS 1+, Android 8+, Windows 10+, and Linux. Apple Watch is recommended for biometric features.

Is Echoelmusic a medical device?

No. Echoelmusic is NOT a medical device and is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease. It is designed for general wellness, relaxation, and creative expression only.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription through your device's App Store settings. On iOS: Settings > Your Name > Subscriptions > Echoelmusic. On Android: Play Store > Menu > Subscriptions.

Is my biometric data private?

Yes. All biometric data is processed on your device and never sent to our servers unless you explicitly enable cloud sync. We never sell your data. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Can I use Echoelmusic without an Apple Watch?

Yes! The app includes a comprehensive Demo Mode that simulates biometric data. You can also use other compatible wearables or manually input values.

What is HRV coherence?

Heart Rate Variability (HRV) coherence is a mathematical measure of the regularity of your heartbeat intervals. Higher coherence is often associated with relaxation and focused states. This is used for creative purposes in the app, not medical diagnosis.

How do I report a bug?

Please email support@echoelmusic.com with details about the issue, your device model, OS version, and steps to reproduce the problem.

Is there a refund policy?

Refunds are handled by Apple App Store or Google Play Store according to their respective policies. Contact Apple/Google support directly for refund requests.
