FAQ

Apple Watch not connecting?

Enable Bluetooth. Go to Settings > Privacy > Health and grant permission.

Audio latency issues?

Reduce buffer size in Audio Settings. External audio interfaces recommended for lowest latency (5.33ms).

Biofeedback not responding?

Check HealthKit permissions. Apple Watch must be worn for live HRV data.

Face tracking not working?

Grant camera permission. Face ID required for ARKit face tracking on iPhone.
