Frequently Asked Questions

Apple Watch not connecting?

Enable Bluetooth. Go to Settings > Privacy > Health and grant permission to Echoelmusic.

Audio latency issues?

Reduce buffer size in Audio Settings. External audio interfaces recommended for lowest latency (5.33ms).

Biofeedback not responding?

Check HealthKit permissions in Settings. Apple Watch must be worn for live HRV data.

Face tracking not working?

Grant camera permission in Settings. Face ID required for ARKit face tracking on iPhone X or newer.

How do I export my creations?

Use the Share button in any session to export audio, video, or project files to Files, iCloud, or other apps.

Is my data safe?

Yes. All biometric data stays on your device. We don't collect or store any personal data. See our Privacy Policy.
