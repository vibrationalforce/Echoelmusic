Introduction

Echoelmusic ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS application.

TL;DR: All data stays on your device. No servers, no tracking, no ads. You own your data.

Information We Collect

1. Health & Fitness Data (HealthKit)

What We Collect:

  • Heart rate (BPM)
  • Heart rate variability (HRV)
  • Workout data (duration, type)
  • Movement and activity metrics

Why We Collect It:

  • To create bio-reactive music that responds to your physiological state
  • To map heart rate to tempo and musical parameters
  • To provide real-time biofeedback during music creation
  • To enable wellness and meditation features

How We Use It:

  • All health data is processed locally on your device
  • Health data is NEVER uploaded to our servers
  • Health data is NEVER shared with third parties
  • Data is used only for real-time audio parameter mapping
  • No health data is stored permanently

2. Microphone Access

What We Collect: Real-time audio input from your device microphone

Why: To record audio for music production and enable live audio effects processing

How We Use It:

  • Audio is processed in real-time for recording and effects
  • Recordings are stored locally on your device
  • Microphone data is NEVER transmitted to our servers
  • No voice recognition or speech processing occurs

3. Camera Access

What We Collect: Camera frames for face tracking and video recording

Why: To detect facial expressions for audio parameter control and to record music videos

How We Use It:

  • Face tracking is processed locally on your device
  • Camera data is NEVER uploaded to our servers
  • Videos are stored only in your photo library (with your permission)

4. Photo Library Access

We only write to your photo library (save files). We do not read existing photos without explicit permission.

5. Bluetooth Access

Used only for connecting:

  • Bluetooth MIDI controllers
  • Bluetooth heart rate monitors
  • Bluetooth audio devices

No location tracking via Bluetooth. No data transmission to Bluetooth devices except audio/MIDI signals.

6. Motion & Orientation Data

Used for head tracking (spatial audio) and device movement detection. All processing is local.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Your name, email, or personal identity
  • Your location or GPS data
  • Your browsing history
  • Your contacts or messages
  • Analytics or usage statistics
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Any information about other apps on your device
Echoelmusic is a privacy-first application. We do not have servers that collect your data.

Data Storage & Security

Local Storage Only

All your data is stored exclusively on your device:

  • Music sessions and recordings
  • Instrument presets
  • App settings and preferences
  • Project files (.echoel format)

No Cloud Services

  • We do not operate cloud servers for user data
  • We do not sync your data to the cloud
  • We do not store your data remotely

Data Sharing & Third Parties

We DO NOT share your data with third parties. Period.

  • No data brokers
  • No advertisers
  • No analytics companies
  • No social media platforms
  • No government agencies (unless legally required)

Your Rights & Control

Permissions Management

Permission How to Manage
Microphone iOS Settings → Echoelmusic → Microphone
Camera iOS Settings → Echoelmusic → Camera
HealthKit Health app → Sources → Echoelmusic

Data Deletion

  • Delete all app data: iOS Settings → Echoelmusic → Delete App
  • Delete specific projects: Inside the app, swipe to delete
  • Clear all sessions: App → Sessions → Edit → Delete All

Legal Compliance

We comply with:

  • GDPR (European Union) - No data collection, full user control
  • CCPA (California) - No data sale, complete transparency
  • PIPEDA (Canada) - Privacy by design, minimal data collection
  • LGPD (Brazil) - Data protection and user rights

Children's Privacy

Echoelmusic does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is rated 4+ and is safe for all ages. No personal information is collected from any user, regardless of age.
